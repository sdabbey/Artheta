    Who We Are

    ArTheta Inc. is a pan-African company dedicated to reshaping the future of African communities. Our mission is to build sustainable, affordable, and culturally-grounded environments that promote green mobility, smart infrastructure, and community-driven development across the continent.

    Our Vision

    A future where every African community is innovative, connected, sustainable, and deeply rooted in its unique cultural identity.

    Our Philosophy

    We believe that Africa’s future should be shaped by its own values, traditions, and aspirations, combined with innovative, forward-thinking solutions. Our goal is to create holistic, self-sustaining communities that empower all generations across Africa.

    How we're realising our vision

    BusangaApp

    Smart Bikes Smarter Health Greener Campuses

    Ride Smart, In Style, and Fit!

    It's more than just transportation; It's a movement towards sustainability, fitness and freedom. BusangaApp is Artheta Inc.’s flagship mobility and wellness project — a cycle-sharing platform built for students and gated communities across Africa. It’s more than just transportation; it’s a movement toward sustainability, fitness, and freedom. With GPS-enabled bikes and built-in health tracking (heartbeat, weight, and calories), BusangaApp helps you move smarter and live better — all through a simple monthly subscription. We’re kicking things off this August 2025, offering students affordable, eco-friendly rides and the tools to track their wellness on the move. Partner with us to make African mobility sustainable. Be an ambassador today — ride, earn, and inspire change.
